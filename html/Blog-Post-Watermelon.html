SUMMER: Watermelon

Aug 17, 2020

Be as cool as a watermelon! - Eggtart

During summer, it is the watermelon season. I especially like seedless watermelon since you won't feel the texture of the seeds when you are enjoying it. Watermelon is around 90% water, which can keep me hydrated during the hot weather. However, you would be consuming a lot of sugar if you eat too much watermelon. My mom usually cuts the watermelon in half and scoop over it watching TV show. I will approach her to see if I can luckily get a chance to drink the juice. Sometimes she would put some into my little bowl, but most of the time she keeps it to herself and let me sit watching her eat.

Author Info

Eggtart is an orange tabby cat born in shelter. He is very affectionate and love petting. He is interested in exploring new territory and taking pictures with his friends.
