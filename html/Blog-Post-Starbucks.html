Starbucks Special

Jul 10, 2020

Sometimes you just need the cup! - Eggtart

With every season comes a whole new line-up of Starbucks specialty drinks. In 2021, spring brought pistachio lattes and brown sugar oatmilk espresso. Summer meant strawberry funnel cake Frappuccinos and mango dragonfruit refreshers. Fall saw the cult-favorite pumpkin spice lattes and apple crisp macchiatos. And, last but not least, winter will bring peppermint mochas and caramel brûlée lattes. But the beverages aren't the only seasonal specialties that Starbucks' fans look forward to — they also wait all year for the new merchandise releases.
